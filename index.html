01 / Who I Am

Researcher.
Engineer. Educator.

I am a Data Scientist and AI Researcher from Bangladesh with an MSc in Applied Statistics & Data Science from Jahangirnagar University (CGPA 3.92/4.00, WES-evaluated 3.97/4.00). My research sits at the crossroads of machine learning, healthcare AI, and statistical modelling.

Currently serving as Senior Research Associate at Data Insightopia and Research Assistant at Pabna University of Science and Technology, I have published 7 peer-reviewed works — three IEEE conference papers, two international journal articles, and two papers under review — spanning Healthcare AI, deep learning, and statistical modelling.

Alongside research, I have spent over 5 years guiding O and A Level students through Mathematics and Statistics — with a track record of transforming struggling students into confident problem-solvers.

Whether you're struggling to pass or aiming for an A*, I tailor every session to your specific gaps, pace, and Cambridge syllabus requirements.
